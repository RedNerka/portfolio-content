DLCs

Father's Promise

Father's Promise, released on November 15, 2017, is the first complete DLC of This War of Mine after the main body of the game was released. It is also the first complete story in This War of Mine, story mode. The DLC told a story between a father and a daughter. Adam and his seriously illed daughter, Amelia was trapped in Pogoren. While the coldness of winter and the lack of meds were quickly taking Amelia's life, Adam has to scavenge for food and meds, reinforce the shelter, keep the house warm all by himself. The DLC revealed what a war could bring to a normal father, and the great, undestructable love of a father.

The Last Broadcast

The Last Broadcast, released on November 14, 2018, is the second complete DLC of This War of Mine. It is the second complete story in This War of Mine, story mode. The DLC told a story of a radio host and his wife. Malik was a disabled radio host, and Esma was his wife. Since Malik is confined to one single floor in the shelter and cannot go out for scavenging at night, he can only make broadcasts to the whole city, to spread the news, to play musics, and to cheer people up. At the same time, Esma is responsible for scavenging and seeking for news and information throughout the city. However, some people like to hear the broadcasted truth, others don't...

Fading Embers

Fading Ember, released on August 6, 2019, is the final complete DLC of This War of Mine so far. It is the third complete story in This War of Mine, story Mode. The main character is a young girl called Anja. Her grandfather who has gone away has a collection of artworks and cultural relics. However, the blizzard makes Anja quickly consumes the stored fuel. The player should make a decision whether to burn the artworks for survival or protect these artworks and keep the record of civilization.

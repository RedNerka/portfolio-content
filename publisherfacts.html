Publisher & Fun Facts

Publisher

11 Bit Studios is a game development company founded in September 11, 2010. The company is located in Poland. In their official website, they give video games a completely different definition from the conventional idea. "We consider games to be a form of storytelling. Our games expand on this by offering ambiguous choices, evoking emotions that have been reserved for other forms, tackling topics not previously seen in games, and simply looking for new ways to offer worthwhile experiences." And indeed, This War of Mine, as one of the most well-known games produced by 11 Bit Studios, has depicted war from a brand new angle, namely, from a civilian's view.

Fun Facts

Thanks to the distinctive style of its games, 11 Bit Studios has won several awards.

  • Nominated for the Seumas McNally Grand Prize in Independent Games Festival (IGF), 2015, and won the Audience Award with This War of Mine.
  • Nominated for Best Strategy Game at the Game Critics Awards, 2018.
  • Won the Strategy Title of the Year at the Australian Game Awards, 2018
  • etc.

Besides the award, there are some other fun facts about 11 Bit Studios and This War of Mine.

  • This War of Mine generated a revenue that is equal to the total development cost two days after its release.
  • On March 10, 2015, 11 Bit Studios released a charity DLC for This War of Mine. The company claimed that it would donate all the revenue from the DLC to help the kids who were sufferring from the Iraq War. As reported by the company on April 3, 2015, it had helped 350 Iraqi kids in rescue, fundamental education and psychological assistance. The four countries which have the most DLC buyers are USA, German, UK, and Russia.
  • In 2020, This War of Mine was officially added to the recommended reading list in Polish high schools. This was the first attempt in the entire world that a game is added to the reading list of high schools. The game could be downloaded for free from the Polish government website on Windows OS.
  • In 2022, 11 Bit Studio claimed to donate all the revenue generated by This War of Mine between February 24, 2022 to March 2, 2022 to the Ukrainian Red Cross. About $850,000 in total was donated.
